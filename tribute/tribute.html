Roberto Firmino

Liverpool's #9

Here are some facts about Roberto Firmino:

  • 2014 – present: Named as part of Brazil's international team.
  • 2015: Debuted for Liverpool against Swindon Town on August 2.
  • 2015: Scored first goal for Liverpool against rivals Manchester City on November 21.
  • 2015–2016: Ended season as Liverpool's top scorer in league.
  • 2017–2018: Officially took on #9 after signing of Mohammad Salah.
  • 2019–2020: Assisted Liverpool in winning EPL title for 2019-2020 season.
